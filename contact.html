Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the questions clients usually ask before we begin.

What does Grid Velocity do?

Grid Velocity is a production, design and web studio for brands and their agency partners. We work across branding, video, websites and paid ads — helping teams get good ideas made, launched and working properly.

Are you an agency?

Not in the traditional sense. We're a studio built to work flexibly with brands, agencies and internal teams. Sometimes we lead the work. Sometimes we support quietly in the background. The point is to help get the work done properly.

Do you work directly with brands and with agencies?

Yes. We work with brands directly, and we also partner with creative agencies, production teams and marketing teams that need reliable support across design, web, video or paid ads.

Can we hire you for just one service?

Yes. You can hire us for a defined project — like a website, logo, campaign film or Shopify store — or for ongoing monthly support across creative, web and digital work.

How does a project usually start?

It usually starts with a short call or brief. We understand what you need, ask the right questions, define the scope, timeline and cost, and then get to work once everything is aligned.

Do you offer fixed pricing?

Yes, for defined-scope projects. Once we understand the work, we share a clear quote with the scope, deliverables, timeline and payment terms.

Do you offer monthly support?

Yes. Monthly support is useful for brands or agencies that need regular website updates, design help, creative assets, ad support or small ongoing improvements without hiring a full internal team.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope. A small design or landing page project may take a couple of weeks, while a full website, brand identity or video production may take several weeks. We confirm timelines before the project begins.

Who owns the final work?

Once the project is complete and paid for, you receive the agreed final deliverables and source files, subject to any third-party licensing terms.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. We work with clients across different markets and time zones, including Pakistan, the Middle East, North America and beyond.
