Year2025
ServicesPersonal Branding, Web Design, Web Development
Timeline7 weeks

The Brief.

Claudia Monroy was already producing exceptional weddings for high-end clients. What she lacked was a brand and digital presence that matched the level of work she was doing — something that could open international doors and pre-qualify the right clients before the first call.

We designed a personal brand rooted in elegance and restraint: a refined wordmark, a warm neutral palette, and a typographic system that whispers luxury rather than shouting it. The website architecture puts her portfolio front and centre — no filler, no stock, just real work. An inquiry flow optimised for conversion without feeling transactional.

Six new bookings in her first month live. Four international client referrals in the first quarter. The brand attracts exactly who she was already doing her best work for.
