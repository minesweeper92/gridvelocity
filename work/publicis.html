Year2024
IndustryAdvertising, Marketing
ServicesUX Design, Responsive Design, CMS Integration, Webflow Development
Live SiteVisit ↗

The Brief.

RED Publicis needed a responsive, easy-to-manage website — a significant upgrade from their existing dated design. One of Pakistan's most recognised advertising agencies deserved a digital presence that actually reflected the quality of their work.

The challenge was threading the needle between aesthetics and functionality: create something visually engaging and user-friendly, while integrating a CMS robust enough that their team could keep it fresh without developer support — all without compromising brand alignment.

We mapped user flows and built a responsive design that adapts cleanly across every device. The Webflow CMS integration made content updates genuinely effortless. We also restructured the content architecture to align tightly with RED Publicis's brand, giving the site a sharper editorial voice that matches the agency's own standard of work.
