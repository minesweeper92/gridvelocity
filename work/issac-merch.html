The Brief.

ISSAC Merch is the official merchandise store for the film Love Me Dead. We designed and developed a custom Shopify experience that translates the film's dark, emotional aesthetic into a functional, scalable e-commerce platform — purpose-built to convert fans into customers.

The challenge was cinematic fidelity meeting e-commerce pragmatism: the storefront needed to feel immersive and on-brand while still being easy to navigate, mobile-friendly, and set up for seamless purchasing. A heavy, moody aesthetic and a clean purchase flow don't naturally coexist — making that work was the design problem to solve.

We delivered a custom Shopify build with bespoke sections, integrated payment gateways, and a mobile-first layout throughout. The result is an immersive storefront that balances storytelling with performance — the film's dark aesthetic wrapping a shopping experience that actually works.
