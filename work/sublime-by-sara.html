Websites

Sublime by Sara

ClientSublime by Sara
Year2025
ServicesWeb Design, E-Commerce Development, Photography Direction
Timeline6 weeks

The Brief.

Sublime by Sara makes occasion wear for women who want to look genuinely exceptional — not just dressed. The challenge was an e-commerce site that converts without feeling transactional. The luxury category punishes anything that looks like it was templated, and their customers know the difference.

We designed around editorial photography first: the product deserved to be seen properly, not squeezed into a grid. A warm, jewel-toned palette and refined typography create the boutique atmosphere, while the UX flow — from collection landing to checkout — removes every unnecessary step. Photography direction ensured the imagery matched the site's visual language from the start.

It doesn't feel like a website. It feels like a boutique. And the numbers backed it up within the first month.
