Enkay Consulting

ClientEnkay Consulting
Year2025
ServicesBrand Identity, Web Design, Web Development
Timeline8 weeks

The Brief.

Enkay Consulting had the expertise and the client list but a brand that wasn't keeping pace. The ask was clear: bring the visual identity up to the level of the work they were already doing, and build a website that could open doors before a single conversation started.

We rebuilt the identity from the ground up — a refined mark, a corporate colour system grounded in depth and trust, and a type hierarchy built for credibility. The website followed the same logic: clear service architecture, a content hierarchy that guides senior decision-makers, and a performance baseline that holds on every device.

Eight weeks from brief to live. The new brand communicates exactly who Enkay is — credible, sharp, and ready for growth.
