The Brief.

Enkay is a Dubai-based financial consultancy with a growing commodities trading arm. They brought us on board to give their brand a strong, modern identity and create two distinct but connected digital experiences — one for their consultancy, and one for their trading division.

The brief came with a real challenge: they had no visual identity, no brand direction, and needed two websites that felt related but not identical. The goal was to look sharp, global, and trustworthy — without falling into the usual corporate trap of safe-but-forgettable design.

We built a bold, clean logo and visual system flexible enough to flex across both companies. That extended into a full brand kit — stationery, decks, digital assets. Then we designed and developed two sleek websites with consistent UX and visual language, while tailoring the tone and focus for each business line. Sharp where it needed to be, approachable where it counted.
