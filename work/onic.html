The Brief.

ONIC came to us as a fast-moving challenger ready to look the part. The mandate: build a brand that feels unmistakably theirs, scales across every surface, and earns trust at first glance — in a market already full of noise.

We ran a full brand workshop, explored three distinct visual directions, then committed to a bold, high-contrast identity system: a flexible logo mark, a confident colour story, and a type voice that carries from billboard to button. The launch film series followed the same energy — each cut built to stop a scroll and start a conversation.

The result is a complete ecosystem: identity, motion, social content and living brand guidelines that keep everything sharp as the team grows.
