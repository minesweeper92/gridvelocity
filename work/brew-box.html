Brew Box Concept

ClientBrew Box (Concept)
Year2025
IndustryFood & Beverage
ServicesLogo Design, Brand Identity Concept, Typography Exploration

The Brief.

Brew Box was a proposed specialty coffee concept built around an industrial, container-style setup. We were brought in to develop an early logo direction that could define the brand's tone and set a clear visual character before the concept moved into development.

The brief required a careful balance: the identity needed to feel bold and industrial while retaining genuine warmth and approachability — and it had to work seamlessly across signage, cups, packaging, and future applications without losing either quality.

We designed a logo concept inspired by industrial signage and modern café culture, with a strong emphasis on typography and versatility. The mark felt confident, contemporary, and rooted in craft — a clear foundation for wherever the brand might grow. This was concept work; the final identity was not implemented.
