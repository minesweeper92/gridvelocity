# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Business owners, brand managers, marketing directors, and creative leads who need production-grade creative work: brand identity, video production, website builds, paid ad creative. They arrive with a brief or a budget problem, not as learners. They're evaluating whether Grid Velocity is the right partner by reading the site. A significant portion arrive via referral or social media having already formed an impression; the site either confirms or kills it.

## Product Purpose

Grid Velocity's website is the studio's permanent pitch. It doesn't describe the work — it IS the evidence. A prospective client finishes a page visit and either thinks "these people are the real thing" or moves on. Every section, transition, and line of copy is an audition. Success is a qualified inquiry, not a bounce.

## Brand Personality

Kinetic, authoritative, committed. Grid Velocity operates at the intersection of production value and commercial strategy: a studio that moves fast and delivers work with cultural weight. The voice is direct and confident without being cold. It has taste, and it shows.

Three-word personality: bold, precise, alive.

## Anti-references

- Generic Pakistani agency sites: stock-photo hero, "we are passionate about your brand", navy/blue/purple palette, clip-art icons.
- Bloated portfolio sites: 12-column case study card grids, identical service cards, lorem-ipsum scale filler.
- Over-animated micro-agency sites that compensate for thin creative with excessive scroll effects.
- The editorial-magazine aesthetic lane (display serif + italic drop caps + broadsheet grid): a saturated lane, not Grid Velocity's register.
- SaaS design language: dark mode + purple gradients + glassmorphism + "boost your productivity" copy.
- Light-mode "modern creative agency": salmon accents, Instrument Sans or Plus Jakarta Sans, hero image with heavy overlay text.

## Design Principles

1. **The site is the work.** Every section passes the brand slop test on its own merits. Nothing ships that a competitor could have made.
2. **Kinetic restraint.** Motion is a production tool, not decoration. Animations are purposeful: they reveal, pace, or emphasize — never just scroll effects for their own sake.
3. **Color leads.** The orange (#EF4823) is not an accent — it's a voice. When it appears, it commits. Neutrals exist to make the orange louder, not to soften it.
4. **Typographic authority.** Boldonse at scale is the brand's strongest weapon. Use size contrast aggressively. Archivo handles the rest with precision.
5. **Dark for conviction.** Grid Velocity works under stage lights, on set, in dark venues. The site belongs in that world — dark is not a default, it's a deliberate claim about who this studio is.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA on all pages. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for every animation. Semantic HTML first, ARIA only where HTML falls short. All interactive elements keyboard-navigable with visible focus states. Contrast ratios verified against actual computed colors, not approximated.
