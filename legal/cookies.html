Cookie Policy

Last updated 7 June 2026

At M/S Grid Velocity, we believe in being transparent about how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences when visiting www.gridvelocity.com.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, improve user experience, and provide insight into how visitors use the site.

Cookies may be classified as:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic website functionality
  • Performance / analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Functionality cookies — remember preferences and improve user experience
  • Targeting / advertising cookies — support advertising, retargeting, and campaign measurement

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Ensure our website functions as expected
  • Improve website speed, performance, and usability
  • Understand how visitors navigate our website
  • Analyse traffic, engagement, and website performance
  • Support marketing activity and measure campaign effectiveness
  • Remember cookie preferences where a consent tool is enabled

Some cookies may be set by third-party services used on our website.

Cookie Consent and Control

When you visit our website, you may be presented with a cookie banner or consent tool where applicable. This may allow you to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customise your cookie preferences.

You can also manage or withdraw cookie consent through your browser settings or through the cookie preference tool on our website, where available.
