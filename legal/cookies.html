Cookie Policy

Last updated 25 May 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make sites work more efficiently and to provide information to site owners. We also use browser local storage — similar in purpose to cookies but stored in a different part of your browser.

2. How We Use Cookies

We keep our cookie use lean and purposeful. The Site currently uses:

  • Strictly necessary storage — to remember your cookie consent choice so we don't ask you again on every visit.
  • Analytics cookies (consent-gated) — only activated if you click "Accept All" in our cookie banner. These help us understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it. No analytics cookies are set until you give consent.

We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking cookies or profiling cookies.

4. Third-Party Scripts

Some pages on the Site load JavaScript libraries (animation and video tools) from content delivery networks (CDNs). These are pure script deliveries — they do not set cookies or track you. The CDN providers may log your IP address for delivery and security purposes, subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Managing & Withdrawing Consent

You can change your cookie preference at any time:

  • Via your browser: Most browsers let you view, manage and delete cookies and local storage through their settings. Clearing your browser's local storage for this site will remove the gv_cookie_consent entry and the consent banner will reappear on your next visit.
  • Via browser extensions: Tools such as uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger let you block specific cookies and trackers across all websites.
  • Device settings: Mobile devices also offer privacy settings that limit cookie tracking across apps and browsers.

Please note: blocking or deleting essential local storage entries may affect how the Site works (for example, the consent banner will reappear every visit).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we change how the Site uses cookies. We will update the "Last updated" date above when changes are made. We encourage you to check back periodically.

7. Contact

Questions about cookies or your privacy? See our Privacy Policy or email us at hello@gridvelocity.com.
