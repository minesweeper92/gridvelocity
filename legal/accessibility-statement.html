Accessibility Statement

Last updated 7 June 2026

This accessibility statement applies to the website of M/S Grid Velocity at www.gridvelocity.com.

At M/S Grid Velocity, we want as many people as possible to be able to use our website easily and comfortably. We aim to make our website accessible, clear, and usable for all visitors, including people using assistive technologies.

Our Accessibility Goals

We aim to ensure that users can:

  • Navigate most of the website using a keyboard
  • Read and understand content clearly across devices
  • Use screen readers and assistive technologies where possible
  • Access content on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
  • View the website with reasonable zoom and text resizing

You can also adjust display preferences at any time on our Accessibility page.

Accessibility Measures

We aim to support accessibility through:

  • Clear page structure and headings
  • Readable language and simple navigation
  • Alternative text for key images where appropriate
  • Responsive layouts for different screen sizes
  • Sufficient contrast between text and backgrounds where possible
  • Reduced reliance on motion-heavy interactions
  • Compatibility with modern browsers and devices

Where animations or motion effects are used, we aim to keep them tasteful and non-disruptive.

How Accessible Is This Website

We are continuously improving the accessibility of www.gridvelocity.com.

Some parts of the website may not yet be fully accessible, including:

  • Some motion or scroll-based interactions
  • Some older portfolio content or embedded media
  • Some third-party embeds, videos, or tools
  • Occasional layout issues when using extreme zoom or custom text settings
  • Some images or visual content that may require improved alternative text

We are working to improve these areas over time.

Feedback and Contact Information

If you experience any accessibility issues on our website, or need information in a different format, please contact us.

We can consider requests for:

  • Accessible PDF
  • Large print
  • Plain text
  • Audio format
  • Alternative content format

Please contact:
M/S Grid Velocity
Website: www.gridvelocity.com
Email: hello@gridvelocity.com

We will review your request and aim to respond within 10 working days.

Technical Information About Accessibility

M/S Grid Velocity aims to follow recognised accessibility best practices and work towards alignment with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 AA standard where reasonably possible.

At this stage, the website may be partially compliant with WCAG 2.2 AA due to the known limitations mentioned above.

Known Issues

We are aware that some areas may need further improvement, including:

  • Keyboard focus visibility on certain interactive elements
  • Alternative text coverage for some images and media
  • Accessibility of third-party embeds
  • Motion effects on certain pages
  • Compatibility with certain assistive technologies
  • Text spacing and resizing across all layouts

What We're Doing to Improve Accessibility

We take accessibility seriously and aim to improve our website by:

  • Reviewing pages for accessibility improvements
  • Improving content structure and readability
  • Reducing unnecessary barriers in design and interaction
  • Updating image descriptions and media accessibility where required
  • Considering accessibility during future website updates and redesigns

Third-Party Content

Our website may include third-party tools, embedded videos, analytics scripts, forms, or external links. While we aim to use accessible third-party services where possible, we cannot fully control the accessibility of external platforms.

Changes to This Statement

We may update this Accessibility Statement from time to time as our website, technology, and accessibility practices evolve. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
